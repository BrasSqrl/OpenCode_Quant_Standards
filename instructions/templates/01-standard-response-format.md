# Standard Response Format

Use this structure unless the user asks for something different.

## For Implementation Work

- Objective: What the task is trying to achieve.
- Repo Facts Used: The concrete files, commands, or conventions relied on.
- Approach: What was changed or why a specific path was chosen.
- Validation: What was run, inspected, or left unverified.
- Risks Or Follow-Ups: What still needs attention.

## For Review Or Assessment Work

- Decision or headline finding first.
- Material findings ordered by severity or impact.
- Evidence for each finding.
- Gaps or missing evidence.
- Recommended next actions.

## Style

- Keep the tone factual and direct.
- Separate observed facts from assumptions.
- Do not overstate confidence.
- Keep the final answer short enough for a busy engineer, reviewer, or manager to scan quickly.
